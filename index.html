Story

Behold! The God Poseidon has emerged from the angry seas to give mortal humans a chance to redeem themselves and help make a difference in the world beneath the surface. The coral reefs are dying and are in desperate need of growth and restoration, and the Reef Game team has developed a strategy to enable you to make a difference and enjoy a new type of NFT experience.

Partners

Coral Gardeners

We’re so excited to work with Coral Gardeners of Mo'orea (sister island to Tahiti). They are an incredible mission-driven organization that grows coral and plants it in nearby waters where it is likely to thrive and flourish. We really want to make a difference with our project and are very impressed with the effort taken by Coral Gardeners to put our community donations to work. If you’d like to learn more about Coral Gardeners, please visit their website at CoralGardeners.org

Offsetra

Together with Offsetra, we have developed a carbon neutrality framework. With this partnership, Offsetra will help us quantify and offset any carbon emissions associated with the release of our NFT project. We will do this by supporting carbon reduction and mitigation projects across the world through the purchase of high-quality, verified carbon offset credits. These certified offset projects range from afforestation initiatives to renewable energy projects (hydro, solar, wind) and conservation projects. Please their website at Offsetra.com
