Story

Behold! The God Poseidon has emerged from the angry seas to give mortal humans a chance to redeem themselves and help make a difference in the world beneath the surface. The coral reefs are dying and are in desperate need of growth and restoration, and the Reef Game team has a developed a strategy to enable you to make a difference and enjoy new type of NFT experience.
