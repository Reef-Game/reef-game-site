Not your average Otter

Reef Game Otters are a collection of 2,500 utility-enabled PFPs that feature a vastly diverse and unique pool of rarity-based traits. What's more, each Otter unlocks exclusive benefits in the Reef Game project and additional benefits the longer you hold them.

A collection built to reward our community members and earliest advocates

The Reef Game Otter Collection is our thanks to our early adopters of the Reef Game underwater metaverse. Earn exclusive early access, pre-mint capabilities, benefit from Otter Specific community giveaways and earn unique Reef Game items.

Mint Details / Pricing

All OG Divers and Reef Game Whitelists will be able to mint 1-2 Otter PFPs for free (excluding gas fees). The unminted portion will be made available to the public for a cost of .03 ETH.
